About this website!

Two avid programmers, Jacob Meksavanh and Hani Asim, set up this website for firms to figure out the patients who should receive COVID vaccines first.

Currently, the priority rating is computed using a (very) simple formula created by Jacob. He wishes to further develop this formula by referencing the organ allocation priority formula used by hospitals in the US.
